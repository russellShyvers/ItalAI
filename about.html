WHO WE ARE

A research-first
AI organization.

Founded in 2024, ItalAI operates at the intersection of academic research and industrial application.

We collaborate with leading universities, research centers, and technology partners to advance artificial intelligence, and translate that knowledge into deployable systems. Our work spans fundamental research, applied AI, and system-level design, with a strong emphasis on correctness, reliability, and scalability.

We do not position ourselves as a traditional AI consultancy. Our role is to invent, engineer, and validate AI systems designed to function beyond experimental settings.
